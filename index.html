About the System

The Ocean Hazard Monitoring System is a comprehensive platform designed to monitor, analyze, and provide early warnings for ocean-related hazards affecting coastal regions.

Features

  • Real-time hazard monitoring and reporting
  • Social media integration for crowd-sourced data
  • Advanced analytics and trend analysis
  • Multi-language support
  • Mobile-responsive design
